Cilo Sat Lakes Camp

Ikiyaka Cilo Sat Lakes is a good alternative for nature lovers looking for a Hakkari camping area and Yüksekova camping area. Especially when Hakkari province is combined with its natural beauties and friendly people, it appears as a unique tourism route of our country. The presence of camping facilities, bungalows and free natural camping areas in addition to the hotels in the city pleases visitors who want to go beyond the classic hotel holiday. In this sense, İkiyaka Cilo Sat Lakes is an option where you can have a good time, especially for the concept of rest and holiday close to nature, which has increased its popularity in our country recently. Sat Glacial Lakes fascinate people with their untouched nature and offer drinking water to the local people.

Climbing Mount Sümbül in Hakkari

It's a challenging climb to Mount Hyacinth at 3,487, which is similar to the Swiss Alps. Mountaineers enjoying tea on the top of the hyacinth at the summit with a bird's eye view of Hakkari can have their photos taken day and night. The aim here is to introduce the mountains and natural beauties of Hakkari to the public of Turkey and the world.

EXPLORE THE HAKKARİ

Hakkari is the sixty-second most populous province in southeastern Turkey. As of the end of 2020, it has a population of 280,514 people. All of them are located in the east of the Eastern Anatolia Region. It is the forty-eighth largest province of Turkey in terms of surface area. It is bordered by Iraq in the south, Şırnak in the west, Van in the north and Iran in the east. The population of Hakkari province is 280,514. (end of 2020). The area of the province is 7,095 km2. There are 40 people per km2 in the province. There are 5 districts, 8 municipalities, 56 neighborhoods and 125 villages in these municipalities.
